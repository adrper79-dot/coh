# Cypher of Healing Experience Gap Audit and Plan

## Current Gaps

### Identity Gaps

- The site sounds evocative, but visitors still have to infer what Cypher of Healing actually is.
- The doctrine exists in source material, but most visitors never see the brand thesis.
- The five-stream model is present, but the relationship between streams is not explained clearly enough.

### Trust and Compliance Gaps

- The current experience does not clarify the non-therapy boundary early enough.
- Legal and trust links in the footer are placeholders.
- There is no durable public page for doctrine, orientation, or informed participation.

### UX Gaps

- The homepage is atmospheric before it is orienting.
- New visitors do not get a clear answer to who this is for, how to enter, or what step to take first.
- Conversion paths are present, but the order of trust-building is weak.

### Copy Gaps

- Some copy is strong but too poetic to carry critical understanding on its own.
- Some pathways still read like product shells rather than steps in one restoration journey.
- The booking, academy, and events journeys need clearer expectation-setting.

## Experience Plan

### Phase 1

- Publish the founder identity and compliance language internally.
- Add public-facing doctrine and disclaimer routes.
- Rework the homepage so orientation comes before abstraction.

### Phase 2

- Clarify each stream with explicit role, expected outcome, and best-fit audience.
- Replace dead legal and trust links.
- Tighten CTA sequencing around first-time visitors.

### Phase 3

- Build dedicated pages for show, books, and founder story.
- Add social proof tied to specific outcomes, not only atmosphere.
- Add email capture and guided funnel paths based on visitor intent.
