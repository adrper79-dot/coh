# Cypher of Healing Compliance Language

## Short Site Disclaimer

Cypher of Healing offers reflective wellness experiences, education, and restorative products. We do not provide therapy, diagnosis, medical treatment, or crisis services through this platform.

## Booking Disclaimer

Barber-chair sessions may include reflective conversation, wellness-oriented guidance, and personal-development themes. These sessions are not psychotherapy, counseling, or a substitute for licensed mental health care.

## Academy Disclaimer

The Academy provides educational material for personal reflection and growth. It is not therapy, clinical treatment, or emergency support.

## Events Disclaimer

Workshops, webinars, and community gatherings are educational and community-based. They are not group therapy or clinical care.

## Crisis Language

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, thoughts of self-harm, or need urgent support, contact 988, call emergency services, or reach out to a licensed local provider immediately.

## Credentialing Guardrails

- do not use therapist, counselor, clinician, psychotherapy, treatment, or diagnosis language unless legally accurate
- do not imply licensure in copy, forms, product descriptions, or event naming
- prefer reflective care, wellness guidance, educational framework, restorative conversation, and personal-growth language
